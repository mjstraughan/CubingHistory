2. FMC 222 - wrong commutator
From: "Bruce Norskog" <brnorsk@...>
To: fewestmoveschallenge@yahoogroups.com
Date: Tue, 06 Jan 2009 18:51:34 -0000

In his FMC 222 "classic" solution, it appears to me that Eric picked the correct insertion point, but chose the wrong commutator! The commutator D L D' R2 D L' D' R2 would have given a 28f solution instead of 29f: U L' B D2 R' U' L U' B' U L2 F2 D F' D2 F' L D' R2 D L' D' R2 F2 D' F' D2 F (I note that this exact same solution could also be achieved inserting a commutator one move earlier.)
